The Startup Checklist

This is a list of everything you need to do to launch a startup. At Atomic Squirrel, we believe in the power of the humble checklist, so every time we launch something new, we consult this list to make sure we didn't forget any important steps. Did we set up Google Analytics? Is our website showing up in Google Search? Are we tracking expenses correctly? Did we set up social media accounts and a mailing list? Is the database backed up regularly?

Much of this list is based on the book Hello, Startup, which is a comprehensive guide to building products, technologies, and teams. Please note that this list is always a work in progress. It covers the most common tasks you'd need to do at just about every startup, but the startup world is constantly changing, and every startup is a little different, so some things may not apply to your startup and some things that your startup needs may be missing. The list is open source, so we welcome any corrections, updates, and additions on GitHub.

We hope you find this list useful for launching your startup. If you need help with any of the items below, you can submit your list to Atomic Squirrel at the bottom of the page, and we'll quickly respond to discuss how we can work together.
