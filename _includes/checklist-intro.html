The Startup Checklist

This is a list of everything you need to do to launch a startup. At Atomic Squirrel, we believe in the power of the humble checklist, and after using this one for years to successfully launch a variety of projects, we're excited to share it with all of you. Every time you launch something new, consult this list to make sure you didn't forget any important steps. Did you set up Google Analytics? Is your website showing up in search results? Are you tracking business expenses correctly? Did you set up social media accounts and a mailing list? Is your database backed up regularly?

Please note that this list is always a work in progress. It covers the most common tasks you'd need to do at just about every startup, but the startup world is constantly changing, and every startup is a little different, so some things may not apply to your startup and some things that your startup needs may be missing. The list is open source, so we welcome any corrections, updates, and additions on GitHub. Also, the list doesn't go into much detail on each task, so for a deeper look, check out the book Hello, Startup: A Programmer's Guide to Building Products, Technologies, and Teams.

We hope you find this list useful for launching your startup. As you fill out the checklist, your progress will automatically be saved in your browser. You can also print the checklist at any time. If you need help with any of the items, or just need feedback and advice, you can submit your list to Atomic Squirrel at the bottom of the page, and we'll set up a free 30 minute call to discuss your startup.
