About

At Atomic Squirrel, our mission is to make it an order of magnitude easier to understand, develop, and deploy software. We are a team of DevOps experts who specialize in creating high quality, battle-tested, end-to-end infrastructure, and we want to help you launch and scale your startup.

Our most common services include:

  • Infrastructure-as-code (e.g., Terraform, Docker, CloudFormation)
  • Automated deployment (e.g., blue-green, rolling, canary, feature flags)
  • Security (e.g., VPC, Security Groups, SSL/TLS, Auth, Vault, KMS)
  • Architecture (e.g., 12-factor, microservices, high availability, scalability)
  • Tech stack (e.g., server-side framework, client-side framework, data storage)
  • Build pipeline (e.g., continuous integration, Jenkins, CircleCI, Travis)
  • Asset pipeline (e.g., concat, minify, fingerprint, CDN)
  • Product development (e.g., design, rapid prototyping, validation, distribution)
  • Monitoring & alerting (e.g., CloudWatch, Logstash, DataDog, PagerDuty)
  • Training (e.g., talks & workshops, code & design reviews, documentation)

Let us take care of the technology so that you can focus on what really matters: your product and your customers.
